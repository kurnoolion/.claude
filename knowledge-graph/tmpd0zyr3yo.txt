The main approaches:

1. **Cloud-based remote desktop solutions** - These work through firewalls because they use outbound HTTPS connections (port 443), which most corporate firewalls allow. Examples: Chrome Remote Desktop, TeamViewer, AnyDesk, Parsec, RustDesk with relay.


2. **VPN-based solutions** - Tailscale and ZeroTier create mesh VPNs that can tunnel through NATs and firewalls using relay servers.
3. **Traditional VPN + RDP** - Less likely to work through corporate firewalls.
